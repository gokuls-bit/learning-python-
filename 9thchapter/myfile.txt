Samarkand, a jewel of the Silk Road, boasts a rich history spanning millennia. This Uzbek city is famed for its stunning Timurid architecture, including the Registan Square with its magnificent madrasahs, the Gur-e Amir mausoleum, and the Bibi-Khanym Mosque.
